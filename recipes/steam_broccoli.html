How to Steam Broccoli

This is how to steam broccoli on the stovetop, preserving its gorgeous green color, texture, and nutritional value. It's easy to make using a steamer basket, and the notes below provide two additional methods.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds broccoli crown, cut into 1-inch florets
  • Olive oil, salt, and ground black pepper to taste (optional)

Directions

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Fill a large saucepan with 1 inch of water.
  3. Step 3: Add a steamer basket to the saucepan; bring to a boil over high heat.
  4. Step 4: Place broccoli florets in steamer basket; reduce heat to medium-low and cover.
  5. Step 5: Steam until broccoli is crisp tender, 5 to 6 minutes, or desired doneness.
  6. Step 6: Season with olive oil, salt, and pepper, to taste, as desired.

Cook's Note:

How to steam broccoli without a steamer:
Add 1/2-inch of water to cover the bottom of a large pot with a lid. Bring to a boil.
Add broccoli florets and cover.
Steam for 5 to 6 minutes until broccoli is crisp tender or desired doneness.
Season as directed in recipe.
